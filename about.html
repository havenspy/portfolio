About Us

Spy is a platform dedicated to the improvement of security in our country. It provides a complementary means for reporting cases of insecurity.
At Spy, we believe everyone has the power to make his/her environment safe and secure. Hence, we are giving you a means to utilize that power.

OUR MISSION

To make Nigeria safer and more secure for economic development and growth; and to enhance the quality of life in Nigeria by working in partnership with the community to enforce the law, preserve peace, reduce fear, and maintain order.

OUR VISION

To collaborate with the public and security agencies in the collection and dispensation of information with the intention of ensuring the safety and security of the country ,to provide an alternative means of reporting incidents to the security agencies and ensuring immediate help ,to build the confidence of Nigerians in her security agencies.
